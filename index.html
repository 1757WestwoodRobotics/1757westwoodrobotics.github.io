OUR MISSION


Founded in 2006, we are a high-school robotics team from Westwood, MA with a mission to inspire young people to become leaders in science, technology, art, engineering, and mathematics by engaging them in mentor-based programs that build important technical and life skills such as leadership, innovation, communication, and self-confidence which are necessary survival skills in today's technologically dominated world.
